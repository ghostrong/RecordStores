Belgrade

Tranquil & relaxed downtown spot by day, bustling & teeming hub by night, with spectacular coffee, great choice of beers and splendid wine selection, where one can buy a good record too.

### Yugovinyl

Cetinjska 15
Belgrade
Serbia

The biggest and best in Belgrade. A lot of different LPs / 7", many genres, nice selection, cool and homey atmosphere and cheap to moderate prices. A must see if you're in Belgrade.
